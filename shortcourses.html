Registration

Guidelines

Deadline

The deadline of registration for any short course shall be on the first day of class.

Class Size

  • The minimum class size offering is 12.
  • The maximum class size is 32, but may be lower in certain specialized classes.
  • UPSITF reserves the right to cancel or postpone any short course offering if the minimum number of students required in a short course class has not been reached.

Withdrawal/Cancellation

  • If a person opts to withdraw his/her registration from any short course, a refund of 80% of training fee shall be made by UPSITF to the registrant.
  • No refund shall be given if a class has already started at the time the registration is withdrawn.
  • In the event that a cancellation of a short course class is made by UPSITF, full refund shall be made by UPSITF to the registrants of said short course class.

Short Course Deliverables

Short Course participants shall receive the following items from UPSITF:

  • 1:1 PC to student ratio
  • course materials
  • Certificate of Completion (must pass the Short Course requirements)
  • snacks and lunch

Payment

  • UPSITF accepts payments in cash, manager’s check, personal check and through credit cards (e.g., MasterCard, Visa).
  • Full payment must be made by the first day of class.
  • Payments made at least a week before classes start is eligible for Early Bird Discount.
  • Only those who paid in full shall be considered enrolled in the class.

Reservation

  1. Make a deposit to the bank account of UPSITF in the amount of Php 500.00. Bank details are as follows: Account Name: UP System IT Foundation Inc. Account Number: 3072-1003-60 Name of Bank: LANDBANK UP Diliman Extension
  2. Fax the Deposit Slip to UPSITF through fax no. (+632) 920-2036.

You may call Ms. Rhea Francisco-Samonte at (+632) 4362217 or (+632) 920-2080 to verify the fax receipt of the Deposit Slip.

Requirements

Individuals interested in taking up a particular short course must submit the following requirements:

  • Duly-accomplished Short Course Application Form
  • One (1) color ID picture (2×2)
  • Php 500.00 Reservation Fee
  • Short course Training Fee
  • Proof of having taken the course pre-requisites (if applicable)
