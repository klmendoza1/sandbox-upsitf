Other Services

CCT Corporate Customized Training

UPSITF customizes its training modules according to the needs of its clients and partners.

TBC4K Tech Boot Camp for Kids + Teens

TBC4K aims to introduce students to a wide array of ICT courses especially designed for kids and teens, including Robotics, Programming, Digital Arts, Blogging, and many more.

ICT4D ICT for Development

ICT4D Philippine Network is a loose network of academe partners that aims to engage government, IT industry, and developmental institutions in contributing to sustainable inclusive growth of the country.

+ Consultations

UPSITF offers consulting services and expertise for the following: Systems Audit and Portfolio Management, Executive Information Systems, Network Design and Administration, E-Learning, and Software Systems and Application Development

Events

Youth Congress on Information Technology

The Youth Congress on Information Technology, commonly called Y4iT, is an enabling event for today’s youth that recognizes young Filipinos as key drivers in the dynamic IT industry.

Y4iT Research Summit

The Y4IT Research Summit aims to foster dialogue about the current IT state of the Philippines, as well as to identify various societal issues and provide potential solutions through the use of Information Communication Technology.

SYNC UP

SYNC UP gathers college student leaders of IT-related organizations and provides them an opportunity for learning through talks from industry leaders, as part of Y4iT’s goal in empowering the youth.

Making IT Happen

The UP System Information Technology Foundation (UPSITF), Inc. is a non-government organization focused on the application of Information and Communication Technology for development. It partners with the UP Information Technology Development Center (UP ITDC) in providing various services such as Education Development, Research & Development, Software Engineering, and IT Project Management, among others.
